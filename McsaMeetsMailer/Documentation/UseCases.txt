-= Use Cases =-

1. Leader adds Meet to spreadsheet.
1a. Leader can view a page which highlights any problems with her Meet(s).

2. Convenor emails out the Full Schedule.
2a. Convenor can view a preview of what the email will look like.
2b. Convenor can view a preview which highlights missing/invalid required fields.
2c. Convenor can edit a message which will preceed the schedule in the email.
